**Funding:** University of Minnesota and Norwegian University of Life Sciences (2026–2027)  
**Role:** Project lead

A networking grant fostering collaboration between NMBU and the University of Minnesota to advance our understanding of Mucoromycota — a diverse and ecologically important fungal phylum. The project focuses on genomics and gene regulation in these fungi, which include both beneficial mycorrhizal partners and opportunistic human pathogens.
