**Funding:** Norwegian University of Life Sciences (2026–2029)  
**Role:** Project participant

BRIDGE brings together researchers across NMBU to build a coordinated research environment around host–microbiome interactions. The initiative supports collaborative projects, shared infrastructure, and training activities that span aquaculture, plant biology, and human health.
