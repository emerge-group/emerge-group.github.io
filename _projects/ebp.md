**Funding:** Norwegian Research Council (2021–2025)

The Norwegian contribution to the global Earth Biogenome Project, aiming to sequence and annotate the genomes of all eukaryotic life in Norway. The project generated high-quality reference genomes for a range of Norwegian species, building a national biodiversity genomics resource.
