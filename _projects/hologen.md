**Funding:** EU-MSCA Doctoral Network (2024–2027)  
**Roles:** 
- Hvidsten, project lead
- Sandve, project participant

HoloGen is a European doctoral training network studying host–microbiome interactions across a range of organisms and disease contexts. The network trains a cohort of PhD students in multi-omics methods, systems biology, and holobiont theory, with the goal of understanding how host genetics and microbial communities co-evolve and co-regulate phenotype.
