**Funding:** Norwegian Research Council (2015–2019)

Used systems genomics approaches to understand the genetic basis of omega-3 fatty acid (EPA and DHA) synthesis and tissue deposition in Atlantic salmon. Findings contributed to genomic selection strategies aimed at improving nutritional value in farmed salmon.
