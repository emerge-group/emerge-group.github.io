**Funding:** Norwegian Research Council (2020–2024)

Addressed sustainability challenges in Norwegian salmon aquaculture by characterising how feed composition shapes the gut microbiome and, in turn, host health and performance. The project integrated metagenomics, transcriptomics, and nutrition science to identify feed strategies that improve fish welfare while reducing environmental impact.
