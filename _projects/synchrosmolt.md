**Funding:** Norwegian Seafood Research Fund (2020–2022)

Investigated the genetics and physiology of synchronised smoltification to improve timing and consistency in commercial salmon smolt production. The project combined transcriptomics with breeding data to identify genetic markers predictive of smolt quality.
