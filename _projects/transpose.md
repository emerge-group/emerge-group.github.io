**Funding:** Norwegian Research Council (2018–2022)

Explored the role of transposable elements (TEs) as drivers of genome evolution and gene regulation. The project examined how TE activity shapes chromatin accessibility, gene expression, and the emergence of novel regulatory elements across vertebrate lineages.
