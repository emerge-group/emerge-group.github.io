Rewired

Whole genome duplication in vertebrate adaptation

Funding: Norwegian Research Council (2018–2022)

Investigated how whole genome duplication rewired gene regulatory networks in salmonid fish, enabling novel adaptations during the colonisation of cold, high-latitude environments. The project produced foundational resources for salmonid genomics and identified key regulatory innovations linked to the salmonid-specific WGD.
